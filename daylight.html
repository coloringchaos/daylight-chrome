This daylight clock provides a dynamic expression of time based on the period of daylight for your current location.

How it works: The hand rotates 360 degrees over the course of daylight hours. Because the sun rises and sets at a different time every single day of the year, the hand of this clock moves at a different speed everyday - it rotates faster in the winter and slower in the summer. The hand does not move when the sun is down.
